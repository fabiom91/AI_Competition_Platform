Automated Evaluation Measures

Select the appropriate evaluation measures for your competition. Our server will use them to score users submissions automatically and generate the Public Leaderboard. It is Your duty to manually evaluate at least the first 10 submissions and assign them a definitive score.

The evaluation measures Weight must be a number between 0 and 1. When a submission is uploaded, our algorithm multiply the corrispondent measure result from the submission by the weight of the evaluation measure you choose. The Leaderboard position is calculate based on the sum of each evaluation measure score obtain by partecipant with its best submission. To know more, check out the FAQ section in the About page.
